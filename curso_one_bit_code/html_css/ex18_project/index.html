WELCOME TO ONEBITSPORTS

This is where you can find all the newest and most fashionable items in your favorite sport.

boxing

Boxing is a combat sport and martial art, in which fighters use only their fists, both for defense and for attack. Boxers exchange blows for a predetermined time over a number of rounds in the ring.

Crossfit

CrossFit is a general strength and conditioning training that provides the widest physiological adaptation for any type of person, regardless of age or physical level, thus generating a greater optimization of all physical capacities.

Swimming

Swimming is the practice of competitive swimming in a pool, involving the four basic styles: crawl, breaststroke, backstroke and butterfly.

Calisthenic

Calisthenics is a set of physical exercises in which you use your own body weight, with or without adding extra weight. Seeks to move muscle groups in a natural way, without using dumbbells and the like.

Basketball

Basketball is a collective sport played by two teams, which aim to score points by hitting the ball in the opponent's basket. The practice is characterized by being dynamic that involves different physical abilities.

Running

Racing is a competition of speed or endurance. Contestants try to complete a given task in the shortest amount of time.

Surf

Surfing consists of gliding over the sea waves with a board, simultaneously performing different maneuvers with different degrees of difficulty..

Trail

trail is a sport that consists of running “off-piste”, on mountain trails or secondary paths, through mountains and hills, crossing streams and rivers, with steep ascents and descents.

Tennis

Tennis is a sport of English origin, played on courts. Two opponents or two pairs of opponents participate in the game, which can be mixed.
